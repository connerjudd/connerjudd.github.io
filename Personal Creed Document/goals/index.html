Goals

Faith: To build my faith I plan to exercise it. I plan to follow the commandments and covenants I have made.

Divine Nature: I want to honor my heritage. I plan to keep my self clean and worthy for the celestial kingdom.

Individual Worth: To understand and fully appreciate my worth I plan to write in a journal everyday.

Knowledge: I plan to finish my degree and to read everyday for the rest of my life to gain knowledge.

Choice & Accountability: I plan to have a planning session each night for the next day. This way I can plan on how I will use my agency.

Service: I plan to be active in my church callings. I plan to be 100% in my ministering assignments. I plan to pray and be actively looking for service opportunities that come up.

Integrity: I try to be slow to answer those around me. This helps me analyze what I am going to say and verify it is what I mean and is the truth.

Virtue: I plan to remove any inappropriate media from my life.
