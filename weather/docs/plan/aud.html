Personas

  1. Cami is a 28 year old student with 2 kids. Cami likes to know the current weather conditions from the safety of her home so she can dress her children appropriately. Cami uses her mobile device.

  2. Conner is an outdoor enthusiast and loves to go for a morning run. Conner is 32 years old and likes to start his day checking his emails on his desktop.

Scenarios

  1. Carly is 5 years old and in kindergarten. She hates being cold and likes to look at the weather on her amazon tablet to know how to dress each day.

  2. Alice is 21 years old and is an avid hicker. She loves to hit the trail regardless of the weather but likes to pack as light as possible. She checks the site daily on her desktop to know what to bring with her.

  3. Zoey is 45 years old and teaches a yoga class for senior citizens at her local park. She checks the site daily on her apple tablet. She cancels class if the temp falls below 65 degrees.

  4. Jake is 19 years old and currently serving a mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints. He does not have a car and walks everywhere he goes. He doesn't like to have to come home for his umbrella or jacket. He checks the site every morning on his Samsung Galaxy to know what to carry with him.

  5. Nate is 28 years old and is owns a nursery. He has several plants that are very sensitive to temperature. He checks the site several times throughout the day on his ipone to keep his plants alive.

Target Audience

My Target Audience is anyone who lives in the area I will be covering.
